Pwnies Workshops

The Pwnies student organization is organizing a series of workshops related to topics in security. Participants will gain hands-on experience with exploiting particular vulnerabilities and learn techniques & tools applicable to hacking and security in general.

Upcoming workshops include:

  • 2017-12-21 : Breaking GCM; The illegal attack
  • 2018-01-11 : Introduction to shellcode and binary exploitation
  • 2018-01-25 : Automatic ROP chain generation using SMT solvers
  • 2018-02-08 : Automating reversing of binaries using radare2

Please find additional information about the workshops at: github.com/Pwnies/workshops

Do not be afraid to drop by!
